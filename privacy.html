PrintSync for Etsy

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2, 2025

PrintSync for Etsy ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when using PrintSync:

  • Etsy Account Information: When you connect your Etsy shop, we receive your shop ID, shop name, and user ID through OAuth authentication.
  • Product Data: Product listings, images, descriptions, prices, inventory levels, and other data you choose to upload or sync.
  • Order Information: Order details, customer information (as provided by Etsy API), and transaction data for fulfillment purposes.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our service, including features accessed and actions performed.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Create and manage product listings on your Etsy shop
  • Sync inventory and product data between your systems and Etsy
  • Process and fulfill orders through your connected fulfillment providers
  • Send you technical notices, updates, and support messages
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our application

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Etsy: We transmit data to Etsy through their official API to manage your shop and listings.
  • With Your Print-on-Demand Provider: If you connect a fulfillment service, we may share order information for product fulfillment.
  • For Legal Reasons: If required by law or to protect our rights and safety.
  • With Your Consent: We may share information with your explicit permission.

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmissions use SSL/TLS encryption
  • OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest and in transit
  • We never store your Etsy password
  • Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security audits and updates

5. Etsy API Usage

Our use of the Etsy API is governed by Etsy's API Terms of Use. We:

  • Only request the minimum permissions necessary for our features
  • Comply with Etsy's rate limiting and usage policies
  • Do not cache or store Etsy data longer than necessary
  • Respect Etsy's intellectual property and branding guidelines

6. Your Data Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can request a copy of your data at any time
  • Correction: You can update or correct your information
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Revocation: You can revoke our access to your Etsy shop at any time through Etsy's settings

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services. When you disconnect your Etsy shop or delete your account, we will delete your data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

8. Third-Party Services

Our application may integrate with third-party services (e.g., print-on-demand providers, payment processors). These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@printsync-etsy.com
  • Through our website: printsync-etsy.com

12. Compliance

We comply with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR for European users and CCPA for California residents. For specific requests related to these regulations, please contact us using the information above.
